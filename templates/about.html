We offer a fully integrated service from a fully committed and equipped team, capable of meeting every related need. Regardless of our clients’ requirement, our approach remains the same. Our first priority is to gain thorough understanding of the requirements of our clients’ business and then efficiently dealing with each task as they present themselves. Mcanda Investment has a very knowledgeable and experienced workforce who subscribe to a strong set of values and ethics for our company in various areas as may be dictated by our customers. To us, ‘a happy customer is a successful service’.

  • Our Vision

    To be the most reliable and efficient service provider offering a broad spectrum of related services and products; committed to service, and renowned for our integrity and ability to deliver on time.

  • Our Mission

    To provide sustainable quality products and services to enhance the social and economic empowerment of eMaswati businesses within the Kingdom’s region.

  • Our Philosophy

    Continuously provide quality client service and to ensure that we are part of the client’s growth within a sustainable empowering environment.
